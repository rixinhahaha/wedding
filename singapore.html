Your Guide to Singapore

Everything you need to know for the trip

Getting There

Plan your arrival

We recommend arriving on Friday, December 18 or earlier to settle in and adjust to the time zone (Singapore is 13 hours ahead of PST / 16 hours ahead of EST). Singapore Changi Airport (SIN) is consistently rated one of the best airports in the world — you might not want to leave!

December is peak travel season in Singapore, so book your flights early for the best prices and availability. The flight is roughly 18–19 hours, so plan for a full travel day.

Direct Flights from the US

From New York (JFK/EWR)

  • Singapore Airlines — Nonstop from JFK/EWR, ~19 hrs
  • United Airlines — Nonstop from EWR, ~18.5 hrs

From San Francisco (SFO)

  • Singapore Airlines — Nonstop from SFO, ~17.5 hrs
  • United Airlines — Nonstop from SFO, ~17.5 hrs
Tip: Singapore Airlines is consistently ranked among the world's best airlines and offers the most nonstop options from the US. Set up fare alerts on Google Flights to catch the best deals. Changi Airport also has free city tours for transit passengers with layovers of 5.5+ hours!

Weather

What to expect in December

Singapore is tropical year-round with temperatures between 25–31°C (77–88°F). December falls within the northeast monsoon season, so expect occasional rain showers — usually short but heavy.

What to Pack

  • Light, breathable clothing (cotton, linen)
  • A compact umbrella or rain jacket
  • A light layer for air-conditioned indoors (malls, restaurants, MRT)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses

Getting to the Venue

Dempsey Hill is accessible by car or taxi but not directly by MRT. Your best options:

  • Grab / Taxi — The easiest option. Grab is Singapore's ride-hailing app (like Uber). A ride from most central hotels takes 10–20 minutes.
  • MRT + Bus/Grab — Take the MRT to Botanic Gardens station, then a short Grab or bus 7, 75, 77, 105, or 106.
About Dempsey Hill: The area is a charming enclave of dining, art galleries, and boutiques set among former British military barracks. Arrive early or stay after to explore!

Travel Essentials

Good to know

✈

Visa

Most nationalities enjoy visa-free entry for 30 days. Check with the ICA website to confirm your eligibility.

💰

Currency

Singapore Dollar (SGD). ~1 USD = 1.35 SGD. Credit cards are widely accepted everywhere. ATMs are easy to find.

🕐

Timezone

Singapore Standard Time (SGT) = UTC+8. That's 13 hours ahead of PST and 16 hours ahead of EST. Give yourself a day to adjust!

📱

SIM & WiFi

Pick up a tourist SIM card at Changi Airport (Singtel, StarHub, or M1). Free WiFi is available at most malls, cafes, and MRT stations.

🚆

Getting Around

The MRT (subway) is fast and affordable. Use Grab for ride-hailing. Get an EZ-Link card at any MRT station and manage it with the SimplyGo app for easy top-ups and transit.

🍝

Food

Singapore is a food paradise! Hawker centres offer incredible meals for $3–5 SGD. Don't miss chicken rice, laksa, and chili crab.

Places to Explore

Make the most of your trip

Marina Bay Sands & Gardens by the Bay

The iconic skyline. Walk through the Supertree Grove, visit the Cloud Forest dome, and catch the nightly light show at the waterfront.

Hawker Centres

Experience Singapore's famous street food culture. Try Maxwell Food Centre (Chinatown) or Lau Pa Sat (CBD). Affordable and delicious.

Orchard Road

Singapore's premier shopping belt. Miles of malls, department stores, and restaurants. Great for holiday shopping in December!

Cultural Districts

Explore Chinatown for temples and street food, Little India for vibrant colours and spices, and Kampong Glam for the stunning Sultan Mosque and Haji Lane boutiques.

Sentosa Island

A resort island with Universal Studios, beaches, the S.E.A. Aquarium, and plenty of family-friendly activities. A full day trip on its own.

Singapore Zoo & Night Safari

World-class zoo set in a rainforest. The Night Safari is a unique after-dark experience — one of Singapore's must-dos.

Botanic Gardens

A UNESCO World Heritage Site just minutes from the venue. Stroll through the National Orchid Garden and enjoy 160+ years of tropical greenery.

East Coast Park

A beautiful coastal stretch perfect for cycling, rollerblading, or enjoying seafood at the nearby East Coast Lagoon Food Village.

Extend Your Trip

Explore the region while you're here

You're already flying halfway around the world — why not make the most of it? Singapore is a perfect launchpad to explore Southeast Asia. Flights are short and affordable, and December is a fantastic time to visit the region.

🇲🇾

Malaysia

1–2 hr flight · or bus/train

Kuala Lumpur for the Petronas Towers, incredible food, and Batu Caves. Penang for the best street food in Asia. Langkawi for duty-free island beaches. You can even take a bus to Malacca (2 hrs) for a charming heritage day trip.

🇮🇩

Bali, Indonesia

~2.5 hr flight

Temples, rice terraces, world-class surfing, and stunning sunsets in Ubud and Seminyak. An incredibly affordable tropical getaway that pairs perfectly with a Singapore trip.

🇹🇭

Thailand

~2 hr flight

Bangkok for temples, street food, and night markets. Phuket or Krabi for stunning beaches and island hopping. Thai food alone is worth the trip.

🇻🇳

Vietnam

~2 hr flight

Ho Chi Minh City for buzzing energy and pho. Hanoi for the Old Quarter and Halong Bay cruises. Da Nang / Hoi An for lantern-lit streets and beautiful beaches.

🇰🇭

Cambodia

~2 hr flight

Visit the magnificent Angkor Wat temple complex in Siem Reap — one of the wonders of the world. December is dry season, perfect for exploring. Phnom Penh offers rich history and riverside dining.

🇵🇭

Philippines

~3.5 hr flight

Over 7,000 islands to explore. Palawan for crystal-clear lagoons, Boracay for white sand beaches, and Cebu for whale shark encounters and waterfalls.

Tip: Budget airlines like Scoot, AirAsia, and Jetstar fly out of Changi with great deals to most of these destinations. Book early for December travel!
